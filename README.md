VR-ROAD: Immersive VR Driving Simulator

Website: vr-road.com

Demo Video: [YouTube Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DnPFD-KncgM)

Description:
VR-ROAD is an immersive virtual reality driving simulator designed to provide safe, realistic, and structured exposure for individuals experiencing driving anxiety. The system integrates a VR headset, force-feedback controls, and interactive driving scenarios to help learners practice real-world driving tasks without physical risk. It supports progressive difficulty, enabling users to build confidence, skill, and familiarity with common driving situations.

Virtual Driving Environments

The simulator includes five structured environments arranged to gradually increase task difficulty and support learning:

Calibration & Familiarization: Learn basic controls (steering, acceleration, braking) and adapt to the VR environment with no traffic.

Open Road Driving: Practice lane keeping, speed regulation, and navigation on traffic-free roadways.

Traffic Interaction: Engage with dynamic traffic scenarios, responding to other vehicles while maintaining safe driving performance.

Nighttime Driving: Simulate low-visibility conditions with headlights and traffic to increase cognitive and perceptual demands.

Parking & Low-Speed Maneuvers: Focus on precise steering, spatial judgment, and control in parking scenarios—important for novice and anxious drivers.

These environments form a progressive learning sequence, moving from basic control acquisition to complex, realistic driving tasks. Users advance sequentially, allowing for assessment of comfort, perceived realism, and tolerance across increasing levels of driving demand.

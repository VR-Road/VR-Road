## Description

VR-ROAD is an immersive virtual reality driving simulator designed to provide **safe, realistic, and structured exposure** for individuals experiencing driving anxiety. The system integrates:

- A VR headset (Meta Quest 3)  
- Force-feedback steering and pedal controls  
- Interactive driving scenarios  

It allows learners to practice real-world driving tasks **without physical risk**. The simulator supports **progressive difficulty**, enabling users to build confidence, skill, and familiarity with common driving situations.

---

## Virtual Driving Environments

The simulator includes **five structured environments** arranged to gradually increase task difficulty and support learning:

1. **Calibration & Familiarization**  
   - Learn basic controls: steering, acceleration, braking  
   - Adapt to VR environment with no traffic

2. **Open Road Driving**  
   - Practice lane keeping, speed regulation, and navigation  
   - Traffic-free roadways for low-stress skill acquisition

3. **Traffic Interaction**  
   - Engage with dynamic traffic scenarios  
   - Respond to other vehicles while maintaining safe driving performance

4. **Nighttime Driving**  
   - Simulate low-visibility conditions with headlights and traffic  
   - Increase cognitive and perceptual demands

5. **Parking & Low-Speed Maneuvers**  
   - Focus on precise steering, spatial judgment, and vehicle control  
   - Important for novice and anxiety-prone drivers

> These environments form a **progressive learning sequence**, moving from basic control acquisition to complex, realistic driving tasks. Users advance sequentially, allowing for assessment of **comfort, perceived realism, and tolerance** across increasing levels of driving demand.
